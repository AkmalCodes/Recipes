# Cinnamon Buns Recipe

#### Cinnamon Filling
- 400 g castor sugar  
- 200 g butter  
- 3 tbsp cinnamon powder  
- 30 g vanilla extract  

#### Bread Dough
- 1.1 kg bread flour  
- 15 g yeast  
- 15 g salt  
- 200 g butter  
- 500 g water  

#### Instructions

1. Mix all dough ingredients (flour, butter, salt, dry yeast, water).
2. Knead for 5 minutes, then let rest for 30 minutes.
3. Knead again for 5 minutes, then let rest for another 30 minutes (total resting time: 1 hour).  
   - Skip the second kneading if the dough is already smooth and passes the windowpane test.
4. Shape the dough into a rectangle.
5. Store dough in the fridge for cold proofing (2 hours).
6. Take out the dough and lengthen it.
7. Apply cinnamon butter filling evenly over the dough.
8. Cut the dough into strips and form buns.
9. Proof buns for 3 hours before baking.
10. Preheat oven to 200°C (30 minutes before baking).
11. Bake each batch at 190°C for 15 minutes.
